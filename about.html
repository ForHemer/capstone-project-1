What is new in this year's iteration?

This year's iteration of the Computer Graphics Conference (CGC) will see the launch of a bold plan to move away from the widely limited 3D shader technology. This has been in the works for a while, but after the dust settled following the dramatic revelations surrounding the RTX in NVIDIA's hottest GPU GPUs earlier this year, the community has finally come together to see if they can make real advances with the next generation of GPU technologies.
